FriendsInTech - a free workshop event for young girls focused on female camaraderie in tech.

June 23rd, 2018 / Suffolk Community College - Grant Campus / 9am - 5pm

What is the purpose of this event?

The purpose of this event is to introduce the meaning and importance of STEAM, how STEAM engagement positively impacts communities, and the opportunities it provides young female minorities.
